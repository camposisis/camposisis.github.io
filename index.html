Isis Renee Campos

Laura C Harris Scholar in Residence, Denison Univeristy

Welcome! Isis Campos is currently a Ph.D. Candidate in the Hispanic Studies Department at the University of Houston and an Inter-University Program for Latino Research/ Mellon Fellow. For the Fall of 2020 she has accepted a position as a Laura C. Harris Scholar-in-Residence Postdoctoral Fellow at Denison University. Her research focuses on US Latino/a literature, with a specialization in US Central American literature.
